This is the first entry in the field log. Here I break down engineering and
product decisions — not in their polished final form, but the way they actually
looked while I was making them.

## The format

Every note is numbered. Sometimes it's a post-mortem, sometimes a by-the-numbers
recap of an experiment. The goal is to capture the line of reasoning, not just
the result.

> A brand lives not in a logo but in a voice and a couple of rituals.
